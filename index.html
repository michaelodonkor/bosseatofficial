Available Features

Features available to all registered members to have the best experience tit our app.

Sell Meals

Users can sell their meals for clients to request an orders

Request an order

Users can request an order on a meal published by other users

Track sales

Users selling their meals can track their weekly sales and overall sales.

Track position in queue

Users requesting for an order will be notified on their position when requesting for an order in a queue.

Call Seller or Client

Bother sellers and clients can contact each other when its the clients turn to receive his or her order.

Track location

Both seller and client can track their locations.

About BossEat App

Boos Eat is an app that lets you discover and order meals from local vendors, offering options for quick healthy lunches, hearty dinners, and sweet treats.

Boos Eat delivers nutritious and tasty meals from local vendors to busy individuals. The app makes ordering easy, allowing users to enjoy delicious food without cooking or leaving home.

We support local businesses by making it easy for vendors to publish their meals on our app, Boos Eat. Our platform allows small bakeries, local restaurants, and food trucks to reach a wider audience and grow their business. It's user-friendly and enables quick and easy meal publication to attract new customers.

At Boos Eat, we aim to provide accessible and convenient food for all. Browse and order now to join our satisfied customers.
